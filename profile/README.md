## 🧠 Overview

**It Takes Two** is a 2021 cooperative action-adventure game that requires two players to work together to overcome unique challenges. Players take on the roles of Cody and May, a couple transformed into dolls, navigating imaginative levels filled with puzzles, obstacles, and interactive mechanics. The game emphasizes teamwork, problem-solving, and coordination, offering a fun and engaging experience for friends or couples playing locally or online.
